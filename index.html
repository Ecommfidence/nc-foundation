MISSION STATEMENT
Eagles Cornerback Nolan Carroll established the Nolan Carroll Foundation in 2016 to support educational opportunities and to promote and encourage healthy eating and physical activities for disadvantaged youth. The Foundation will support numerous organizations and programs through grant and scholarship programs. The Foundation will carefully select educational, nutritional and physical activity programs that it can support financially and will considers the grant program a partnership with these organizations. With this mission, the Foundation will broaden the horizons and increase opportunities for disadvantaged youth.


Your financial support will help us make many disadvantaged youth achieve their greatness and help improve their quality of life. I would like to personally thank all who have donated your time, money, and effort into making the Nolan Carroll Foundation what it is today!
